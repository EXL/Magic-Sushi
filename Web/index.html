Magic Sushi

Port of the game from MTK OS to SDL2 and Emscripten.

Controls:

  • Arrows and 4, 6, 8, 2 on Keypad – Move cursor.
  • Mouse Click, Space, Return and 5 on Keypad – Select tile.
  • M and 7 on Keypad – Mute sound.
